how does it work?

  • Enter your card number to check if it's Visa, MasterCard or American Express
  • Press "check" button and take a look at the picture at the right bottom corner
  • All Visa card numbers start with a 4. New cards have 16 digits. Old cards have 13; e.g. 4126758909876543 or 4874906573827
  • MasterCard numbers either start with the numbers 51 through 55 or with the numbers 2221 through 2720. All have 16 digits. E.g. 5227389876456734 or 2225465789356478
  • American Express card numbers start with 34 or 37 and have 15 digits. E.g. 347869864736537 or 375467837645982
